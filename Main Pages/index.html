How It Works

Creators build their digital shop with beauty services they love. They share their unique 4-digit code with local salons. Salons can only sign up when invited with a 4-digit influencer code, which is required for getting access to Hlfwrld. When salons sign up using that code, they gain exclusive access to view and manage their influencer-driven new clients. Creators earn commissions on bookings while salons get increased visibility and authentic content from trusted influencers.
